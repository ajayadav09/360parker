    Parker's Engineered Materials Group designs and manufactures a wide range of molded, extruded and machined solutions. In addition to all of the o-rings the oilfield relies on every day, Parker also manufactures: packer elements, cement plugs, liner wipers, frac balls, expandable liners, mud/frac pump seals and more.

    Always innovating, Parker's HTR Series Unibody Actuators enhance the proven performance and efficiency of the rack-and-pinion hydraulic actuator by creating a compact, environmentally-sealed, modular package. Reduced weight, fewer fittings required and increased corrosion resistance makes this the ideal actuator for oilfield applications.

    Parker is always working on new product solutions to meet the oilfield's needs. Two recent examples include the MCC Series Multicoupler that can accommodate up to 10 connections in a single plate, and the new 59 Series high pressure, high impulse, thread-to-connect quick coupling. Both ideal for today's coiled tubing rigs.

    Black Eagle hoses are multi-spiral, wire-reinforced and specifically designed for oilfield applications. Available in lengths up to 9,800ft with working pressures up to 15,000 psi. Hose construction includes COLORGARD, a dual color safety feature and an extra thick cover for superior abrasion resistance. Black Eagle is 70% lighter than flexible pipe and 45% lighter than rigid pipe.

    Parker offers a range of natural gas related products.     Parker is focused on your equipment's uptime. The IPDR particle detector (available in explosion proof configuration) is designed to be a cost-effective solution to real-time hydraulic fluid monitoring. An integral part of any preventative maintenance program, Parker detection and prevention solutions help you identify problems early and avoid costly downtime.

    Parker isn't only focused on making sure your hydraulic fluid flows properly. Parker Racor provides the solutions to ensure your engine is getting clean fuel, air and oil. In addition to the many on-engine solutions, Parker also offers portable fuel cleaning, transfer and dispensing units

    As an expert in cylinder design and manufacturing, Parker can be relied on for all of your linear actuator needs. Welded, tie rod, compact, custom, telescoping, electromechanical, pneumatic, and more.
